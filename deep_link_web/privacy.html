Privacy Policy for Wish Listy

Last Updated: Dec 30, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Wish Listy. We value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data to help you organize gifts and events.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Info: Name, email, and profile picture.
  • Content: Wishlists, items, event details, and interests.
  • Social: Friend lists and interactions (reservations, invites).

3. How We Use Data

  • To create and manage your wishlists and events.
  • To notify you about friend requests, gift reservations, and event updates.
  • To suggest gifts based on your selected interests.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your wishlists are shared only with friends you approve (or public if you choose).

5. Account Deletion

You can request account deletion at any time from the app settings.
